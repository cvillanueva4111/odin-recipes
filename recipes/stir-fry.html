Stir Fry

Description

This will show you how to make a stir fry.

Kind of making it up as I go but oh well.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Cook the veggies as desired.
  3. Add sauce and veggies to the noodles so the sauce combines with everything.
